Biography


  1. Me

    I was born in Mexicali Baja California, but for a while I lived in Guadalajara Jalisco, I returned to Mexicali to study engineering, and currently I work as a teacher. I have three brothers , two of them are older and one is younger. I also have two nephews and a niece.

  2. My Family

    Twenty-five years ago I married a wonderful woman, I have 3 sons, two of which have finished their professional careers, and one is still studying at the university.

  3. My Hobbies

    Among my hobbies is traveling, and thanks to my work I have had the opportunity to visit many cities in my country, among them the ones I have liked the most are:

    • 1st Toluca
    • 2nd Mexico City
    • 3rd Saltillo
      • .

    I have also been able to travel with my wife and one of the trips we enjoyed the most was visiting the pyramids of Teotihuacan and Acapulco. As Mexicali is a border city, visiting the U.S. is something common, particularly going to Calexico, El Centro, Yuma and San Diego, however I have also been able to visit other cities in the U.S. a little further away, such as Chicago, St. Louis Missouri and Seattle.
